December 27 – 30, 2025 ·

December 27 (NYC Kickoff · Big Bus · Sunset at SUMMIT)

6:00 AM – 8:00 AM
Arrive at Newark (EWR) → Transfer to Manhattan → Breakfast near hotel.
9:00 AM – 9:30 AM
Activate Big Bus (Downtown Loop). 24-hour pass ~$60.
9:45 AM – 12:15 PM
🗽 Statue of Liberty (Ferry from Battery Park). Ticket ~$25–30. Allow 2–2.5 h incl. ferry and museum.
12:25 PM – 12:45 PM
Wall Street Christmas Tree & Charging Bull (photo stop). Free.
12:50 PM – 1:20 PM
🌮 Los Tacos No. 1 (Oculus) — NYC-famous tacos (~$5 each). Quick, fresh & top-rated.
1:30 PM – 1:50 PM
Brooklyn Bridge (entrance viewpoint — optional short walk partway). Free.
2:00 PM – 2:45 PM
📸 DUMBO (Brooklyn) — Hop off Big Bus at Brooklyn Bridge / DUMBO stop. Iconic Washington Street photo with Manhattan Bridge, explore Brooklyn Bridge Park & Pebble Beach skyline view. Optional coffee or snack at Time Out Market New York. Free to explore.
3:00 PM – 3:30 PM
🥟 Vanessa’s Dumpling House (Chinatown) — famous dumplings & sesame pancakes (~$5–8). Hop off Big Bus back to Manhattan.
4:30 PM – 5:45 PM
🌆 SUMMIT One Vanderbilt — day → sunset → night views. Ticket ~$44–55.
6:00 PM – 6:30 PM
🎄 Rockefeller Center Tree & Rink (view free; skating ~$30–45).
6:35 PM – 7:00 PM
Bryant Park Winter Village (free to enter; skate rental ~$18–25). Grab snack or hot chocolate while browsing shops.
7:15 PM – 9:00 PM
✨ Times Square (open 24/7). Enjoy night lights and photo session before returning to hotel.

December 28 (NYC Highlights · Midtown · Temple Evening)

8:30 AM – 9:15 AM
🛍️ Macy’s Herald Square & Holiday Windows (free photo walk).
9:30 AM – 10:15 AM
5th Avenue Holiday Window Walk — Saks, Cartier, and St. Patrick’s Cathedral (free).
10:25 AM – 10:50 AM
Lotte New York Palace — Christmas Tree courtyard (free photo spot).
11:00 AM – 11:30 AM
🚉 Grand Central Terminal — main concourse & ceiling photos (free). Coffee stop inside Magnolia Bakery or Joe Coffee.
11:45 AM – 12:10 PM
🌉 Manila Street (Chinatown) — Filipino bakeries & murals (free walk & photos).
12:20 PM – 12:50 PM
🍕 Joe’s Pizza — classic New York slice (~$5–7). A true NYC must-try lunch.
1:15 PM – 1:40 PM
🍜 Xi’an Famous Foods — hand-pulled noodles / spicy cumin lamb (~$8–13). NYC favorite comfort food.
1:50 PM – 2:20 PM
🏙️ The Vessel (Hudson Yards) — base area photos (free viewing platforms nearby).
2:30 PM – 3:10 PM
🛒 Chelsea Market — browse local shops & artisanal goods (free entry). Optional snack or coffee break.
6:00 PM – 6:40 PM
⛪ Manhattan New York Temple (LDS) — evening visit & photos at 125 Columbus Ave (free entry grounds).
7:00 PM – 7:45 PM
Dinner near Temple: choose quick bites around Columbus Circle or return to Midtown for late night dessert.

December 29 (Drive NYC → Palmyra quick stops → Buffalo check‑in)

6:00 AM – 12:00 PM
Drive NYC → Palmyra (~300 mi · 5.5–6 h). Breakfast on the road.
12:00 PM – 12:30 PM
Palmyra New York Temple (LDS). Free. Photos & quiet visit.
12:35 PM – 1:05 PM
Sacred Grove (Smith Family Farm). Free. Gentle walk.
1:10 PM – 1:30 PM
Hill Cumorah (photo stop). Free.
1:35 PM – 2:05 PM
Book of Mormon Publication Site (Downtown Palmyra). Free.
3:00 PM – 5:00 PM
Drive Palmyra → Buffalo (~95 mi · 1.5–2 h). Check‑in hotel.
6:30 PM – 8:00 PM
Dinner in Buffalo — try Anchor Bar (original Buffalo wings) or Chef’s Restaurant.

December 30 (Niagara Falls U.S. Viewpoints → Lunch → Drive to Virginia)

7:30 AM – 8:15 AM
Drive Buffalo → Niagara Falls State Park (~25–30 min). Park near Prospect/Visitor Center (~$10).
8:30 AM – 9:00 AM
Niagara Falls Observation Tower (Prospect Point). $1.25. Best single panorama (U.S. side).
9:15 AM – 10:00 AM
Maid of the Mist — boat ride (if operating). ~$28 adult. Seasonal; often closed in winter.
10: AM – 11:00 AM
Cave of the Winds — elevator down to the base of Bridal Veil Falls. ~$19–21 adult. Rain poncho & sandals provided. Experience the power of the falls up close!
12:00 PM – 12:45 PM
Lunch near the Falls — Red Coach Inn (year‑round). ~$20–35 pp.
1:00 PM → late evening
Drive back to Virginia (~7–8 h · ~420 mi). Fuel+tolls ~$100–130.
